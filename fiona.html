Fiona

My cat Fiona, deserved her own page dedicated to her on my homepage. She's probably the fattest cat in Norway, old, cranky and lazy, but the best cat in the world nonetheless! As the assignment asks us to create at least one table, i thought a table listing the pros and cons of Fiona would be a perfect way to implement one.
